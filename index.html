DJ.ME

DJME is an automatic djing app which automatically syncs the playlist of people in and sorts them according to the priority which is found by the maximum demand of and priority of song in everyone’s playlist. Now you do not actually require a dj at a party just assign priority of party songs high in your playlist and djme will take care to make your party ultimate fun.
Dj.me allows anyone to host a DJ session yes you heard it right anyone means anyone (Host of a house party , restaurant manager , Friend who’s phone is connected to the music system or a gym instructor at gym) the dj session is Hosted with the help of Wifi you are connected to or your mobile Hotspot . So whenever a person enters the Wifi range or hotspot range his playlist gets synced into the playlist of the host and as many people enter the wifi zone the playlists gets prioritised according to the most common songs in everybody playlist and songs of mutual interests are played.
